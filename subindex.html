Betrayed by the Hype

It was late at night, scrolling through X, when I stumbled upon a crypto project that promised the moon: slick NFTs, a roadmap to riches, and a community buzzing with hype. I poured my savings into it, convinced I was on the cusp of something big. But the dream turned into a nightmare overnight. The developers vanished, the token crashed to zero, and the Discord went silent – a classic rugpull.

The betrayal stung deep. I spent days replaying every red flag I missed: the anonymous team, the vague whitepaper, the too-good-to-be-true promises. I felt foolish, but more than that, I was angry – angry at the scammers who preyed on my trust and angry at a system that let them slip away.

I joined RUGYU, where we turn our scars into strength, exposing scams and building a community that fights back. Now, I’m armed with knowledge and a mission: no more rugs, only roasts! $RUGYU

— Alex
